What is
FOSSCOMM?

A volunteer-run, free-to-attend gathering of Greece's free and open-source software communities — every year since 2008.

FOSSCOMM is a volunteer-run, free-to-attend gathering of Greece's free and open-source software communities. It has been running every year since 2008.

For two days we meet, talk, run workshops, demo what we are building, argue about licences over coffee, and recruit each other into unpaid projects. The conference is free because the volunteers want it to be, and because sponsors make it possible.
